Our story

Harborline AI started when a group of compliance officers and machine-learning engineers kept seeing the same problem: advisors were under pressure to respond instantly, but the tools they used were either too generic or too risky for regulated conversations.

We set out to build something different — an assistant trained for the language of corporate finance, credit assessment, and client suitability, with guardrails designed in from day one rather than bolted on after an incident.

Today, Harborline Advisor supports front-office teams at boutique wealth managers, regional banks, and in-house treasury desks. Every deployment is scoped to the firm’s policies, approved product lists, and escalation paths.

What we believe

  • Human judgment stays in the loop. Our tools draft and research; licensed professionals make the call.
  • Suitability before speed. We would rather pause a response than skip a missing fact about risk tolerance or authority.
  • Transparency builds trust. Clients and regulators should be able to see how advice was formed, not just the final paragraph.
  • Data minimisation is non‑negotiable. We process only what is necessary for the task and delete ephemeral session data by default.

Harborline Advisor

Harborline Advisor is our flagship workspace for regulated Q&A. Advisors paste client scenarios, policy questions, or draft communications and receive structured guidance aligned to internal playbooks.

The Advisor Playground is a live demo environment where prospects and implementation teams can try representative prompts before a formal pilot. Responses in the playground are illustrative and do not constitute financial advice.
